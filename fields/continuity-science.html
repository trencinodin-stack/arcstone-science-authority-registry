Continuity Science

Study of survival, persistence, and existence stability of systems over time.

Core Concerns

  • Existence stability under stress
  • Irreversibility and collapse dynamics
  • Recovery limits and non-recoverable states
  • Long-horizon system survival
  • Failure modes that terminate future agency

Scope

Continuity Science governs whether a system can continue to exist as a coherent, viable entity across time. It evaluates actions and states by their impact on survival and persistence, independent of short-term utility or performance.
