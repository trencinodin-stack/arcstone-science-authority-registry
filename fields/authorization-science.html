Authorization Science

Study of conditions under which actions become legally admissible in a system.

Core Concerns

  • Admissibility theory
  • Permission geometry
  • Fail-closed authorization
  • Certificate-based legitimacy
  • Irreversibility-aware decision boundaries

Scope

Authorization Science governs whether an action is permitted to occur at all, prior to execution, optimization, or learning. It is concerned with legality, not performance; admissibility, not outcomes.
