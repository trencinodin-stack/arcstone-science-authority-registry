Governance Frameworks

Canonical public registry of foundational governance frameworks and doctrines.

  • Existence Admissibility Framework
    Defines criteria under which states, claims, or actions may be considered admissible.
  • Governance State Evaluation Layer
    Classifies system states with respect to governance validity and safety posture.
  • Authorization Constraint Model
    Formalizes authorization conditions and constraint relationships governing action.
  • Legitimacy Assessment Framework
    Evaluates legitimacy of claims, proofs, and authority assertions.
  • Existential Risk Containment Doctrine
    Defines suspension posture under existential or systemic hazard.
  • Interpretability Constraint Principle
    Establishes interpretability as a non-optional admissibility condition.

Registry entries are descriptive and non-operational.
