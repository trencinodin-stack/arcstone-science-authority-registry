# Authorization Field Curvature Law

## Statement
Permission space is non-uniform and deforms under pressure.

## Meaning
External pressures distort which actions appear admissible unless structurally constrained.

## Motivation
Social, economic, and optimization forces bend systems toward over-permissiveness.

## Consequences
- Admissibility must be independently evaluated.
- Pressure cannot be treated as evidence.
- Structural enforcement is required.

## Violations
- Granting permission due to urgency.
- Granting permission due to authority pressure.
- Granting permission due to scale incentives.
